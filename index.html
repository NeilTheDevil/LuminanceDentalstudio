Questions

Common questions, straight answers

The things patients ask us most, answered honestly.

Do you accept new patients? +
Absolutely. We welcome new patients every week. Your first visit is a full 75-minute appointment so Dr. Reyes can get to know you, understand your history, and create a personalized care plan. No rushing, no assembly line.
How much does a first visit cost without insurance? +
A new patient exam with digital X-rays and cleaning runs $199. We're transparent about pricing before any work begins, and we offer 0% financing through CareCredit if you need to spread out larger treatments.
I'm terrified of the dentist. Can you still help me? +
This is one of the most common things we hear, and it's nothing to be embarrassed about. We offer oral sedation and nitrous oxide for patients with dental anxiety. Dr. Reyes also has a "pause anytime" policy. Raise your hand, and everything stops. You're always in control.
How long does Invisalign treatment take? +
Most of our Invisalign patients finish treatment in 6 to 14 months, depending on the complexity. Dr. Reyes will give you a realistic timeline during your consultation, not an overly optimistic guess. We use 3D scanning so you can actually preview your results before committing.
What if I have a dental emergency on a weekend? +
Call our main number. We have Saturday morning hours and keep same-day emergency slots throughout the week. If it's after hours, our voicemail includes Dr. Reyes's emergency line for existing patients. Broken teeth, sudden pain, lost fillings: we'll get you in fast.
Do you offer payment plans for cosmetic work? +
Yes. We partner with CareCredit to offer 0% financing for 6 to 24 months on qualifying treatments. Veneers, implants, whitening, Invisalign — all eligible. We'll walk you through the numbers before you commit to anything.
